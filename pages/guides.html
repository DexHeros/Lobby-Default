Player Guide

Collect characters, play games, and earn rewards from every trade.

What is a DexHero?

A DexHero is a tokenized 3D character — each one is a unique on-chain asset tied to a tradeable token. When people buy and sell the token, a portion of those fees flows directly to holders and game participants.

Your Wallet is Your Account

Connect any BNB Chain, Base, or Ethereum wallet. No sign-up required — your wallet address is your identity, your inventory, and your earnings tracker all in one.

Collecting Characters

  • 1

    Browse the Marketplace

    Head to Trade → All DexHeros to browse every character by price, volume, or most recent. Filter by chain to see only assets on your preferred network.

  • 2

    View the Token Detail Page

    Each character has a detail page showing its 3D model, live price chart, bonding curve progress, total holders, and current tax split. Review this before buying.

  • 3

    Buy on the Bonding Curve or Pool

    Tokens that haven't graduated yet trade on a bonding curve — price rises automatically with demand. Graduated tokens trade on standard DEX liquidity pools.

  • 4

    Hold to Earn Revenue

    As long as you hold tokens, you receive a share of every buy and sell tax collected on that token — distributed automatically to holder wallets.

Playing Games

Find a Game

Go to Trade → All Games to see every game registered on DexHero. Games use DexHero characters as playable assets — if you hold the token, you can use that character in compatible games.

Play-to-Hold Mechanics

Game developers distribute rewards to players who hold their DexHero's token. The longer you hold and the more you play, the more you can earn from in-game events and seasonal rankings.

Earning Rewards

How DexHero revenue sharing works: Every token has a configurable buy tax and sell tax. A portion of these taxes — set by the creator — is distributed to token holders proportionally. Your share = your % of the circulating supply × the holders' allocation.

Soulbound DexHero Play Pass

Want to play games with a DexHero character? Mint the Soulbound DexHero Play Pass at a flat price set by the creator. It's non-transferable by default, locked to your wallet. The only way to permanently unlock it is through compute contribution.

  • 1

    Mint a Play Pass

    Purchase the DexHero's Soulbound Play Pass at the creator-set flat price. Your Play Pass grants access to every game that has whitelisted this character.

  • 2

    Play in Linked Games

    Any game that has whitelisted this DexHero verifies your DexHero ownership on-chain and grants you playable access automatically.

  • 3

    Unlock via Compute

    Contribute bandwidth to the DexHero's P2P network. When your bytes-served counter crosses the threshold, sacrifice your compute credits to permanently unlock the DexHero — making it freely transferable.

Creator Guide

Launch your DexHero, configure revenue sharing, and grow your community.

Overview

Creator economics: As a DexHero creator you earn from every transaction on your token — forever. Set your buy and sell taxes, decide how fees split between yourself, holders, liquidity, and the platform, then sit back while trading activity generates ongoing revenue.

Launching a DexHero

  • 1

    Go to Platform → Create

    Select your target chain — BNB Chain, Base, or Ethereum. Each chain has different gas costs and community characteristics, so pick the one that matches your audience.

  • 2

    Upload or Generate Your 3D Model

    Upload a custom 3D character model (GLB format) or use the AI model generator to create a unique character. The model becomes the face of your token across all DexHero-integrated games and marketplaces.

  • 3

    Set Token Details

    Enter your token name, symbol, description, and social links. These appear on your token detail page and in the marketplace. Good metadata dramatically improves discoverability.

  • 4

    Choose Launch Mode

    Bonding Curve: Token starts with no external liquidity. Price rises algorithmically as buyers come in. Once the curve fills, it graduates to a DEX automatically. Ideal for fair launches.
    Liquidity Pool: Seed the pool yourself with an initial token + SOL/ETH/BNB pair for immediate DEX trading.

  • 5

    Configure Taxes & Deploy

    Set your transaction taxes and fee distribution (see Tax Configuration below), then confirm the deploy transaction in your wallet. Your DexHero is live.

Revenue Sharing

DexHero's revenue sharing system distributes a portion of every buy and sell fee across four buckets. As creator you control the exact split.

Creator Share

The largest bucket — goes directly to your wallet on every transaction. This is your ongoing royalty for creating the DexHero and maintaining its community.

Holder Share

Distributed proportionally to all current token holders. Higher holder allocations make your token more attractive to long-term investors and reduce sell pressure.

Liquidity Share

Auto-compounds back into the trading pool. A deeper liquidity pool means lower slippage for buyers, which supports price stability and makes your token easier to trade at scale.

Platform Share

A fixed platform fee that funds DexHero infrastructure, security audits, and new feature development. This rate is non-negotiable and applies to all tokens equally.

Tax Configuration

Tax strategy matters: Very high taxes (above 10–15% combined) are a common red flag that causes buyers to avoid a token. We recommend keeping total buy + sell tax under 10% for the best community growth. Taxes can be adjusted after launch via Platform → Manage.

DexHero supports separate buy tax and sell tax rates. Each can be configured independently. A common pattern is a slightly higher sell tax to discourage flipping while keeping the buy tax low to attract new holders.

Parameter Range Description
buyTax0 – 25%Percentage taken from every buy transaction
sellTax0 – 25%Percentage taken from every sell transaction
creatorShare0 – 100% of taxYour cut of the collected tax
holderShare0 – 100% of taxDistributed to all holders proportionally
liquidityShare0 – 100% of taxAuto-added to the liquidity pool

Managing Your Token

Update Metadata

Update your token's name, description, image, website, and social links at any time via Platform → Update Metadata. Keeping metadata fresh helps with community trust.

Add & Lock Liquidity

Add additional liquidity to your pool at any time. Lock liquidity for a set period to signal commitment to your community — visible on the token detail page as a trust indicator.

Referrals Program

Enable a referral program for your token. Share your unique link and earn a portion of fees from every wallet that trades through your referral. Track earnings in Platform → Referrals.

Feature Your Token

Purchase a featured slot via Platform → Register Game to promote your DexHero at the top of the marketplace. Featured tokens get significantly higher visibility and trading volume.

Game Developer Guide

Integrate DexHero characters into your game using the REST API.

Overview

The DexHero Game Developer API lets you verify character ownership, fetch 3D model assets, check Play Pass status, and post in-game events — all from your game server or client. Integration takes roughly 30 minutes for most game engines.

Ownership verification
3D model assets
Play Pass verification

Step 1 — Register Your Game

  • 1

    Go to Platform → Register Game

    Connect your developer wallet and submit your game name, description, website, and supported chains. This creates your developer profile on DexHero and makes your game discoverable in the games marketplace.

  • 2

    Receive Your Game ID

    After registration, you receive a unique gameId. Include this in all API requests so DexHero can verify calls come from your registered game.

  • 3

    Generate an API Key

    Open the Developer Portal, connect your wallet, and generate an API key. Keys are scoped to your game and can be revoked at any time.

Step 2 — Authentication

Keep your API key secret. Never expose it in client-side JavaScript or commit it to a public repository. Always make DexHero API calls from your game server. For client-side verification, use the public read-only endpoints which require no authentication.

Include your API key in the Authorization header on all authenticated requests:

Request Header 
Authorization: Bearer dxh_your_api_key_here
Content-Type: application/json

Webhooks

Register a webhook URL in your developer portal to receive real-time push events instead of polling. DexHero will POST a JSON payload to your endpoint within seconds of each event.

token.transfer

Fired when a DexHero token moves between wallets. Use this to update in-game ownership in real time, so players can use a character immediately after purchase.

nft.minted / nft.unlocked

Fired when a Play Pass is minted or permanently unlocked via compute contribution. Subscribe to these events to automatically grant in-game access without requiring players to re-verify.

token.graduated

Fired when a bonding curve token graduates to a full DEX pool. Useful for displaying milestone animations or unlocking special in-game rewards for early holders.

profile.updated

Fired when a player updates their DexHero profile (username, avatar). Sync these changes into your game to keep player names consistent across the ecosystem.

Code Examples

JavaScript / Node.js

// Verify a player owns a specific DexHero before allowing access
async function verifyCharacterAccess(walletAddress, tokenAddress) {
  const res = await fetch(
    `https://api.dexhero.io/v1/characters/${tokenAddress}/owner/${walletAddress}`,
    { headers: { 'Authorization': `Bearer ${process.env.DEXHERO_API_KEY}` } }
  );
  const data = await res.json();
  return data.owns === true;
}

// Fetch all characters a player has available (owned + rented)
async function getPlayerRoster(walletAddress) {
  const res = await fetch(
    `https://api.dexhero.io/v1/wallets/${walletAddress}/characters`,
    { headers: { 'Authorization': `Bearer ${process.env.DEXHERO_API_KEY}` } }
  );
  const { owned, rented } = await res.json();
  return [...owned, ...rented];
}

Trader Guide

Navigate bonding curves and liquidity pools.

Bonding Curves

Tokens trade on a bonding curve before graduation — price is set automatically by the contract based on supply. Every buy raises the price; every sell lowers it. Once the curve fills its collateral target, it graduates to a standard DEX liquidity pool.

Risks

Bonding curve tokens are highly volatile pre-graduation. Assess creator credibility, community activity, and the token's game integration before entering.

Slippage

Large buys move price significantly in a single transaction. DexHero shows estimated slippage before you confirm — split large positions to reduce average entry cost.

Post-Graduation

Once graduated, it trades as a standard AMM token. Key metrics:

MetricWhat It Tells You
Liquidity DepthHow much you can trade without significant price impact.
Locked Liquidity %Higher = creator less likely to pull funds.
24h Volume / MCapHigh ratio = active; can also signal pump activity.
Top Holder %High concentration = centralisation risk.

Reading Signals

Bullish Signals

New game integration, rising holder count, locked liquidity, creator track record, active community, growing volume with stable price.

Red Flags

Unlocked liquidity, anonymous creator, sell tax above 15%, top wallet holding over 30%, no game backing the token, volume spike with no news.
