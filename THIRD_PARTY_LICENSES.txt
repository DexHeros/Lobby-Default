Trust boundary notice
---------------------

The DexHero platform serves forks of this repository from a single
origin (`v3labs.onrender.com`). By forking and modifying this lobby —
either directly or through an LLM brain you have connected to your
DexHero — you accept that any code in your fork executes in the same
browser origin as platform APIs (wallet signing, brain dispatch,
DexHero state).

The platform applies a path allowlist, syntax checks, a static
JS-lint, a per-wallet commit rate limit, and brand-anchor verification
before serving your fork at runtime. These are defense-in-depth
measures; they are not a sandbox.
