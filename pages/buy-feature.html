How It Works

For Game Devs

Register for $100 USDC

Register your game for a one-time $100 USDC fee and add DexHero characters to your allowlist. Your game is hosted on the player-powered P2P network with zero recurring costs — no servers, no subscriptions, ever.

For Players

Deposit Once, Access Everything

Play Pass holders contribute compute power to the DexHero network and help host games for every connected player. Only qualified machines that meet the network's compute threshold are eligible to participate and earn.

The Reward

Earn Permanent DexHero Characters

Contribute enough compute and permanently unlock that DexHero. Hold it for cross-game access forever, or sell it on any marketplace.

Project WarpStream Included

When you register your game, it becomes eligible for Project WarpStream — DexHero's decentralized streaming network. Players with weak devices can stream your game from community GPU servers — no downloads, no hardware requirements. Your game reaches every player, not just those with powerful machines.
