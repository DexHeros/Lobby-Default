Frequently Asked Questions

What internet speed do I need to play?

15 Mbps download for 720p30, 25 Mbps for 1080p60. Most broadband connections work great. We auto-adjust quality based on your connection.

What if there are no servers near me?

You can download the game and play locally. WarpStream is optional — it's available when servers are nearby, and you always have the local fallback.

Is my data safe?

Servers only receive your game input (keyboard/mouse) and stream video back. They never access your wallet, files, or personal data. All sessions are encrypted via WebRTC.

How much can I earn as a server host?

You earn compute-hours while serving real players. After 300 hours (~4 months at 4h/day), you can unlock a Common-rarity DexHero. Or keep providing to unlock rarer, more valuable tiers.

What hardware do I need to host a server?

A high-end discrete GPU (RTX 3080 / RTX 4070 Ti / RX 7800 XT or better) with 12 GB+ VRAM, an 8-core CPU, 32 GB system RAM, NVMe SSD, a wired 50+ Mbps upload connection, and a recent hardware encoder (NVENC Ampere+, AMF RDNA3+, or QSV Gen12+). Our pre-flight check verifies every requirement before your server goes live.

Can I game the system?

Our six-layer anti-fraud system detects self-streaming, fake players, and bot activity. It checks latency, player diversity, input entropy, geographic separation, session quality, and cross-session patterns.
